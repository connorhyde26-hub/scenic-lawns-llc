What We Do

Full-Service Lawn Care for Ogden Properties

From weekly mowing to seasonal cleanups, we keep your lawn looking sharp all year long.

Lawn Mowing & Maintenance

Weekly and bi-weekly mowing, edging, trimming, and blowing to keep your yard consistently clean and healthy. We adjust our schedule with the seasons so your lawn always gets what it needs.

Fertilization & Weed Control

Custom fertilization programs and targeted weed treatments that build thicker, greener turf while keeping dandelions and crabgrass out of the picture.

Aeration & Overseeding

Core aeration relieves compacted soil so water, air, and nutrients reach the roots, and overseeding fills in thin or bare patches for a fuller lawn.

Spring & Fall Cleanups

Leaf removal, bed cleanup, and seasonal prep that gets your property ready for growing season or winterized before the cold hits.

Landscaping & Mulch

Fresh mulch installation, bed edging, and general landscape tidying that adds curb appeal and keeps your outdoor space looking polished.

Sprinkler & Irrigation Checks

Seasonal irrigation start-up, adjustments, and troubleshooting so every zone of your lawn gets even, efficient watering.

About Scenic Lawns

Locally Owned. Dependable. Detail-Focused.

Scenic Lawns LLC is a locally owned and operated lawn care company serving Ogden, UT and the surrounding areas. We built our business on showing up when we say we will, doing the work right the first time, and treating every yard like it's our own.

Whether you need a simple weekly mow or a full lawn health program, our crew brings the same attention to detail to every property — big or small. We believe a well-kept lawn says a lot about a home, and we take pride in helping our neighbors put their best foot forward.

No pushy sales pitches, no surprise fees — just dependable, professional service and a team that actually picks up the phone.
